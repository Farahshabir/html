Ingredients of Chocolate Cake

  • 250 gm all purpose flour
  • 4 beaten egg
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 250 gm powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoon cocoa powder
  • 1 cup butter

How to make Chocolate Cake

  1. Mix all the dry ingredients and pre-heat the oven
  2. Mix wet ingredients into dry ingredients and bake the cake batter
  3. Tasty Chocolate Cake is ready!
